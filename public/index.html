Painting is Fun!

Why do I enjoy painting?

Painting is a hobby I like to do to relax.

It helps me forget about my stress for a little bit because I'm too busy focusing on brush strokes! It helps me turn my brain off and be in the moment. It's my form of meditation!

Other reasons I love to paint:

  • Presents for friends and family.
  • My house is filled with paintings.
  • I sell things that I've painted and make money!
Painting is Fun!!!
